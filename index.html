About me

I’m a front-end developer with a background in graphic design. I’m a recent graduate of the Immersive Web development bootcamp at Juno and the York University/Sheridan College program with a Bachelors in Design.

My background in design allows me to be meticulous in front-end development work and delivering to tight deadlines, as well as creating accessible user experiences. I seek to create experiences that are engaging and valuable to users.
